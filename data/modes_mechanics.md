# Game Modes

## 1. Ladders & What (`standard`)

-   Players roll a die to determine the number of moves.
-   Different question mark squares, called **"Whats"**, have different
    consequences or boosts when landed on.
-   Each character controlled by a player has a unique skill that can
    affect themselves or other players.
-   Players climb ladders when they land on the corresponding connected
    square.
-   The last player remaining on the board loses.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 2. Run Away from WHAT?! (`run_away`)

-   Players roll **two dice** and a **plus (+) or minus (−)** sign. The
    result of the computation determines the number of moves.
-   Different question mark squares, called **"Whats"**, have different
    consequences or boosts when landed on.
-   On a random turn, previously visited squares are destroyed.
-   Players caught during the destruction, or who land on a destroyed
    square, are eliminated.
-   The first player to reach the winning square, or the last player
    standing, wins.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 3. Guess WHAT?! (`guess_what`)

-   No available mechanics yet.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 4. Let's Escape from WHAT?! (`escape_from`)

-   Players work together to escape by reaching the winning square after
    finding the required keys to unlock it.
-   Players and keys spawn randomly across the board.
-   Different entities attempt to stop the players from escaping.
-   Players must use their assigned weapons wisely to fight off the
    entities.
